1、在腾讯云申请域名的证书
2、配置文件


安装相应模块：
yum install mod_ssl openssl　


编辑配置文件：
vi /etc/httpd/conf.d/jerryqi.conf　

内容如下：

<virtualHost *:80>
    RewriteEngine On
    RewriteCond %{HTTPS} !=on
    RewriteRule ^/?(.*) https://%{SERVER_NAME}/ [R,L]
</VirtualHost>
 
<VirtualHost *:443>
    DocumentRoot /var/www/html
    ServerName jerryqi.cn:443
    SSLEngine on
    SSLProtocol all -SSLv2
    SSLCipherSuite ALL:!ADH:!EXPORT:!SSLv2:!RC4+RSA:+HIGH:+MEDIUM:!LOW:!RC4
    SSLCertificateFile /etc/pki/tls/certs/jerryqi.crt
    SSLCertificateKeyFile /etc/pki/tls/private/jerryqi.key
    SSLCertificateChainFile /etc/pki/tls/certs/1_root_bundle.crt
</VirtualHost>　


3、测试并重启服务
sudo service httpd configtest
sudo systemctl restart httpd
